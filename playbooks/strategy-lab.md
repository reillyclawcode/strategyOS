# Strategy Lab Playbook (Skeleton)

**Duration:** 60 minutes

1. **Warmup (5 min)** — prompt participants for horizon + constraints
2. **Map (15 min)** — capture actors, assets, risks via shared canvas
3. **Model (20 min)** — stress scenarios against metrics (growth, resilience, equity)
4. **Decide (15 min)** — pick interventions, log assumptions, assign owners
5. **Close (5 min)** — summarize, export to ops tracker
